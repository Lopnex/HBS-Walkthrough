This is the walkthrough for the game "Horton Bay Stories - Jake" by Lumphorn Games.

This walkthrough is designed to be an aid for the player in which they can see content possibly unknown to them or to show all available content.

New Content will be posted in the Highlight section to the right and once you click a version, it will show what's new in that update.

Current Supported Version: 0.6.1.0

General Information for navigating this walkthrough:

There's a filter button on the bottom right in which you can filter out New Content, Current Storylines, Pregnancy, or NTR. When clicking one it'll change the left side to show the related content to that filter. You'll know if it's in use when the color of the name is changed and both sides have the same color. If you ever wanted to make a section reappear while the filter is on, don't worry just click on Manage Hidden Sections and it'll show all sections that were hidden. Click the one you desire and it'll reappear on the left side until you click on the filters again.

Getting Started!

 The main stats are…

 Stamina – This is used when performing certain actions. Can be replenished by sleeping/resting or ingesting certain items.

 Fitness – Determines how healthy you are overall, and also governs your maximum stamina. Can be increased by physical exercise or ingesting certain items. Can also decrease due to laziness or poor health!

 Intelligence – How smart you are. Can be increased by reading, attending classes, and also by helping the girls with their homework.

 Hygiene – Determines how clean your body is. Certain actions require you to be clean! Can be replenished by washing yourself or using deodorant. Decreases naturally over time and when certain actions are performed.

 Morality – Determines what type of personality your character has been displaying. Certain Objectives or actions are dependent on your Morality. Can be increased by knocking before entering a bedroom & being helpful. Decreases when entering someone’s room without knocking and not being helpful!

 Money – Fairly obvious. You’ll need money to pay for things around the world. Can be obtained by finding, stealing, or working for it.

Relationships

 Every character that you can build a relationship with will have a relationship score. That score consists of the following…

 Friendship – This is your main stat. It determines how much a character likes you as a person and has an impact on how much you can interact with them. Being nice will increase friendship. Being rude or nasty will decrease it.

 Attraction – Characters that are open to having more intimate relationships with you will have an Attraction score as well as Friendship. It determines how much they’ll do with you sexually.

Cheat Mode

 Can be found on your phone under Settings. Activating cheat mode will turn on features to help you manipulate your stats and relationship scores. These can be found on your phone in the relevant sections. If you use Cheat Mode, you do so at your own risk! The game is NOT meant to be played with cheats and some changes can potentially be game-breaking.

YOU HAVE BEEN WARNED!

 However, we do realize that sometimes people get impatient or just want to test things out. So the option is there for those people. With Cheat Mode active, you will see several new options listed below…

 Force Freeroam Toggle – Toggles Freeroam on or off. This is mainly for troubleshooting and is not intended for use in-game. Use at your own risk!

 Stat Decay Toggle – Every weekend, you lose a little intelligence and fitness by default. This option turns that feature either on or off.

 Lucas Toggle – Early in the game, this allows you to make it so Lucas is no longer Christine’s boyfriend, which allows you to see more of her content.

Adel Virginity Toggle

 Adel Virginity Toggle – Allows you to either reset Adel’s virginity, or take it away to open up different content.

AutoHide Location Map

 This toggle simply lets the Location menu stay on the screen after you move rooms when turned off. It’s on by Default. Disabling it can be useful if you want to quickly scan rooms. With the toggle off, when you wish to close the Location Menu just click the ‘x’ at the corner of the screen.

In the case of many games, including this one, this does not strictly affect only your partner/spouse, but also any given love interest or simply someone close to you, such as a family member or a good friend.

Main Story

Explore Horton Bay & discover secrets, scandals, and more. Meet many beautiful girls and potential friends or enemies. The town is here for you to explore!

Meet your new housemates

To complete this objective, visit each of the girls' bedrooms on the upper floor of the house and talk to each of your new roommates. You will also need to speak to Lucas, who is located in Christine’s bedroom.

Find out about the guy who lived in the room before you…

Once you have spoken with everyone and introduced yourself, you learn that the previous tenant of your room was kicked out of the house for being a pervert. To complete this objective, search your room for any information on the previous tenant. You will find Rupert’s Diary in the chest of drawers next to the door. If you also look under the bed there will be some money left from Rupert as well.

Speak to your housemates once more

You’ll find Christine in the kitchen, Adel in the living room, and Gemma in the backyard. To complete this objective, speak to Christine or Adel first and then talk to Gemma.

End of Prologue / Beginning of Episode 1

Spend the rest of the day as you see fit, then head upstairs and go to bed. Episode 1 will begin when you wake up the following morning.

Going to the Book Store

After arriving at the bookshop with Adel, free roam will once again be activated. Before you leave the bookshop, speak to Justin about finding work. Once done, and after you go back home, head to your bedroom.

Optionally, you can buy the plushy toy for Adel to advance her relationship. Justin charges you £300 for the toy. If you have the money you can buy it. Alternatively, he offers you a deal: a photo or video for a discount. There are several ways to obtain a video or photo this early in the game: check Gemma’s bedroom or the recycle bin on your PC.

Wait for the party coming up

After arriving at your room, Lucas will come to see you and tell you about the party on Saturday. During his visit, he’ll make remarks about your mother. Your response will affect Lucas’ friendship stat.

Party Night!

The party on Saturday is split into three stages.

Stage 1: This is simply an introduction scene and there is no choice to be made. Just let the story play out.

Stage 2: Freeroam opens back up and you can wander around the house to speak to everyone. Once you’ve completed all events in all of the rooms, you move on to Stage 3. If you’re stuck, try revisiting all areas of the house.

Stage 3: First, visit the kitchen, followed by Christine’s bedroom. After that, head back to your bedroom. At the end of the night, if you’ve beaten Gemma in a race, you’ll get a bonus scene.

Visiting the local pub

Gemma wakes you up and drags you to the pub with Claire. You’ll head to the bathroom where an event plays out. Then you strike up a conversation with Adel. When she leaves you’ll head back where Jackson has spiked Claire’s drink. They leave together and you trail behind them. A scene occurs depending on your choice, and Claire’s relationship is affected based on what you decide. Regardless, you bring Claire home with you, where you’ll have another choice to make.

First Day at the University

After meeting with the girls downstairs and discussing the events of last night, you all head to the University. When you arrive, all the girls head to their classes and the receptionist Shelly gives you a tour of the school.

If you had sex with Lillie during the party, you’ll get to spend more time with her after Art Class. If you gave Molly a joint at the party, she’ll give you special attention during Science Class.

Saving a damsel in distress! (Nightclub Unlock)

On your way home from Uni, you hear a girl screaming at a nightclub. You recognize the voice as Crystal (the hooker) and run to help her. You end up fighting with Horace (the bouncer) and lose. He then takes you to the club owner’s office. Pie has a conversation with you and you’re recruited for a job you don’t know about. Crystal apologizes for dragging you into her situation.

Meeting Christine at Burger Baps

You visit Christine at her workplace after not talking much since the party. While there, you can talk with Abigail about her and Lucas that night. On your way out, you get a call from Roy (your friend), inviting you over to his home.

Francis meets with Pie

Francis announces his return to Horton Bay and you discover that the two kingpins have a history together. Pie offers a gift to Francis and decides you’ll be delivering it as part of your new debt.

Meeting with Bear

You receive a phone call from Sandra (Pie’s right hand). She instructs you to deliver a box to the Cinema. At the Cinema you’re greeted by Bear, who works for Francis Dean, and he takes the package from you. After a friendly conversation, you leave and are now able to buy drugs at the Cinema during the evenings.

Calling Carly Back

When going to bed, you notice you had a couple of missed calls from your ex-girlfriend, Carly, so you decide to call her back. This scene plays out differently depending on whether the NTR setting is on or off. After you hang up, the girls rush into your room and you’ll have four choices. None will affect the overall story, so pick whichever you like:

  • Go straight to bed – No requirements.
  • Gemma’s scene – Must have completed her story up to the conversation about the pills in the bathroom.
  • Adel’s scene – Must have completed her story up to the bank job.
  • Christine’s scene – Must have completed her story up to the argument with Lucas in the kitchen.

Chatting with Gemma about money

The following morning you find Gemma in the hallway. She receives a phone call with bad news about her finances. You can choose to help her when it comes time for rent. Nothing will happen immediately, but at some point Gemma will come to you.

Jackson Needs a Re-Up

After speaking with Gemma, a scene plays with Jackson’s cousin at Lumptech Biolabs. Jackson phones him asking for more serum but is told there is a price this time.

Adel’s forgotten phone

At noon, while in your bedroom, you receive a phone call from Adel. She’s calling from work and wants you to bring her phone. After you hang up, she has a conversation with Thomas. Depending on previous choices, this can result in the two of them organizing a date to the pub.

If Adel accepts Thomas’ invitation, the next time the clock hits 21:00 you’ll get a text from Lillie. She tells you that Adel and Thomas are at the pub together. You can either rescue Adel or choose to ignore it. If you go to Adel, you’ll be given another choice upon finding them at the pub.

The other choice leads you to leave them be and head downstairs for a drink instead. An hour passes and you’re prompted to go back upstairs if they are no longer at the table. If you explore the area, you find them kissing and Thomas has his fingers inside Adel.

Confronting Jackson

After leaving the bank annoyed, you come across Jackson in an alley behind the bank. You argue with him about what he did to Claire. Realizing he might convince you not to go to the police, he offers you a potential solution to being expelled.

Meet Jackson at Lumptech Biolabs

During the morning, head to Lumptech Biolabs, now available on the town map for fast travel. Speak to the receptionist, who sends you upstairs to meet Dr. Orson Reines, Jackson’s cousin and the man responsible for creating the serum. Jackson and Orson lie about the serum’s true nature and convince you to donate a sperm sample in order to create your own bottle. Afterwards, you and Jackson go your separate ways and your next task is to speak to Miss Smithers, your Head Mistress at the University.

Facing the Music with Miss Smithers

Visit the Head’s office at Uni between 14:00 and 16:00 on a weekday. You are invited in by Miss Smithers and begin to explain yourself when there’s a knock at the door. While Alicia deals with this, you slip the serum into her coffee, which she drinks when she returns. It takes effect instantly and presents you with some options:

Parents Visit

Just after you leave for Uni, your parents arrive on the doorstep. Christine hasn’t left yet and greets them.

If NTR is off, they simply leave a message for you and go on their way.

With NTR on, you have a choice: either let them leave a message, or decide to stay in the house and wait for you to return. Your father doesn’t want to wait around an empty house and decides he’d rather catch up with Nigel. He asks Christine if she’ll show him where the town hall is and they leave the house while your mother waits behind.

After chatting with Christine on the way to the town hall, your father asks if she’d like to hang around a bit longer to get to know his son’s housemates. If Christine’s corruption points are zero, she politely refuses and heads straight to Uni. If they are one or more, she stays to chat more, leading to an awkward moment for them both. Back at the house, your mother searches your room and eventually goes back downstairs to wait. While she sits in the dining room, Lucas arrives.

There are two possible scenarios here:

  • (Alpha Path included) In every other case, he offers your mother a nice cup of “tea”, things get very steamy, and it’s all recorded.

Parents Visit – Part 2

Visit the motel and speak to your folks. You discover that things are less than perfect in their marriage. After being subjected to one of their feuds, you decide to leave and let them try to talk it out in private.

A Trip to the Flicks

Bored at home (your bedroom during the evening), you decide to go to the cinema. On your way out of your room, you run into Christine and invite her to join you. As you arrive, Bear calls you over to speak to Francis, who gives you a job. You are to spy on Pie’s business under the pretense of delivering packages from Francis and eavesdropping on his conversations.

Pie doesn’t trust you enough to let you hang around at first. Keep doing the delivery job until Pie tells you to help Sandra at the bakery for an hour each day. After this, you will be able to stay and listen for a few extra minutes each time and bring intel back to Bear for more money.

Working at the bakery doesn’t grant you pay, as Pie considers this part of your debt being repaid. You do, however, get the benefit of spending time with Bethany and Sandra, increasing their relationships as you do so.

Abrahms comes to town!

After midday, you are prompted to go to your bedroom. A cutscene begins showing that Abrahms has arrived in town with his family, who are not exactly pleased to be there…

Alicia gets a visitor…

Heading to Uni in the morning fires a cutscene showing Abrahms at the Uni reception desk. He has a meeting with Alicia and afterward you’re called into her office so she can warn you about what has just happened. This scene plays out differently depending on your previous choice with Alicia (sub/dom).

Adel, who was too shaken up to recognise Abrahms from the brief moment she saw his picture on the news, leaves you all none the wiser as to his true identity.

Adel's Self Defense Lessons (Part 1)

Realising who Adel is, and deeply regretting his actions, he agrees to volunteer himself for Gemma's task. You will hear a commotion in the garage and find the three of them training there.

If NTR is enabled, you will have the option to allow "Tracksuit" to stay and continue helping, or tell him he can go home and continue in his place.

If NTR is disabled, only the latter may happen.

Before entering the building, Abrahms catches her attention and offers his own services as a trainer. After he convinces her to at least let him try to help, Adel accepts and they head to the beach for their first session.

Jeremy Returns to Town

Another House Party

If both drinks are given to the same person (at different times of the night), extra events may be unlocked (currently in development).

Side Quests

These are quests that don’t have to be done to finish the game. This is just extra content for you to discover and can lead to more spicy scenes!

Rupert’s Cameras

Search the chest of drawers in your bedroom to find Rupert’s Diary. After reading it, check your PC and click on the Cam Viewer icon. To continue this objective, you must choose to spy on the girls. (You will get more chances later if you decline at this point, but you must lower your morality in order to do so.)

Declining at this point grants you +5 Morality, and accepting at any point will grant you -5 Morality. After choosing to activate the cameras, you must find them all. There is one in each room of your house, plus one outside in the back garden near the sauna.

All cameras except for the ones in the girls’ bedroom can be activated immediately when found. To activate the girls’ bedroom cameras you must first find (or guess) the passwords that unlock their PCs. (See “Finding Girl’s Passwords” below.) After activating all cameras you’ll get a live feed on your PC in your bedroom.

Finding the Girl’s Passwords

To be able to access the girls’ cameras you must find their passwords. Once you’ve entered the correct ones you will not need to re-enter them ever again. You have an option to guess it yourself or find it. These are the locations of the passwords:

  • Christine – Bedside drawer in her room.

(Alternatively, when prompted to guess the password, you can type password for all three!)

Rent Day

Every Friday (with the exception of the first week) you have to pay £150 to Nigel for rent. As soon as you wake up, Nigel will come into your room to collect. If you do not pay, he will come back the following week and expect £300 to cover the week you missed.

However, if you do not pay a second time, he will add an additional £50 totaling £500 for the 3 weeks’ worth of rent due. Not paying for a third time will result in Nigel taking “other” steps to get what he is due. At this stage (and for each future missed payment) Nigel will tell you to meet him at the pub and to bring one of your housemates with you.

If NTR is turned on, Nigel will recoup his lost money by allowing the girl that you choose to “pay” for you. If NTR is turned off, you’ll still go to the pub but the girl will leave before Nigel gets a chance to collect.

Doctor Jones’ Camera

After visiting Dr. Jones to get your contact lenses during Episode 1, you notice a camera set up behind a curtain in his office. To view the contents of the camera, you need to sneak into his office while he’s on his lunch break at 12:00 pm.

However, you’ll find that the door is locked while he’s not there. After speaking to the receptionist, you’ll discover that he usually eats lunch on the boardwalk. If the boardwalk location has not yet been unlocked, speaking to Brenda will unlock it now. Go to the boardwalk at 12:00 to find Doctor Jones and his wife.

After a conversation, they leave and you notice his keys on the bench where they were sitting. After picking them up, you will now be able to access his office and the camera while he is not there. Also, having now met his wife, you can speak to her on the beach at 11:00 am every day to begin her relationship arc.

After certain events throughout the game (providing that the requirements for each have been met), Doc Jones’ SD card will start filling up with more recordings.

The University

In order to improve your grades, you must attend at least 4 classes for any given subject per week. You will notice any changes to your grades on the following Monday morning.

Buying textbooks from the Bookshop for any subject will automatically grant you a +1 Grade (a week’s worth of lessons) if you don’t want to spend your time in class. These books can only be purchased once per week, per subject. But they’ll hurt your wallet if you rely on them too much!

Arriving outside Uni at 09:00, you will be given the choice to attend all classes at once, saving you from navigation between them every time. This will advance time to 14:00 after each class has been completed.

4 or more attended classes will increase your grade by 1. 3 classes will render no change. 2 or less will result in losing a grade.

Currently, Uni grades serve no actual purpose. But will play a role as the game progresses. Other methods of gaining grade points are currently in development.

You can currently visit Rhea at the library for help with Maths points, and Stacey at her flat at 21:00 (provided you have progressed her story to the point where she invites you over) for help with English.

During Uni hours (09:00 – 13:00), you can visit the girls’ toilet stalls for random encounters.

The Gym

After attending your first day at University, you will be able to join Didi’s Gym. To start this content you must first speak to Didi. Ask her about a gym membership. She will tell you that it costs £500 for the year, and only staff members get any kind of discount.

You then ask her about getting a job at the gym and she mentions that they currently need a new masseur. If you have not earned any massage skill points yet, you cannot progress further until you do. At this point, if you do not wish to wait, you can still pay the £500 to become a gym member to make use of the facilities. If you have at least 1 point in massage skills, however, you can apply for the position.

Didi will then take you to the massage room in order to judge your skill by letting you massage her. If your massage skill is 10 or more, you’ll get the job. If not, she’ll tell you to come back and try again once you’ve had more practice.

There are already several ways to improve your massage skills outside of the gym. You can go to these locations at those times to improve your massage skill by +1.

Christine (23:00 – Christine’s Bedroom)

Maddy (11:00 – Beach)

Claire (21:00 – Backyard)

You can also read massage skill books from the bookshelf in your room if you have purchased any.

Once employed you can start to earn money from giving massages at the gym between the hours of 14:00 and 22:00.

As a gym member (or employee), you will be able to use the facilities. Any workout at the gym will grant up to a max of 14 points to Fitness (5 more than working out elsewhere). You can currently work out in the Weights Room and by taking yoga classes (17:00) or spar with Gemma (14:00 – Requires Gemma to have a gym membership).

You can also use the Sauna at any time while the gym is open (08:00 – 22:00) as well as the shower in the changing room. There are currently up to 9 random encounters in the Sauna. (Leaving the Sauna and re-entering will trigger a new dice roll.)

Night Club

After saving Crystal from Horrace, the nightclub becomes available to visit from the hours of 18:00 – 02:00. To get in, you need to pay for a ticket at the front desk. This gives you access to the club for that night only.

From 18:00 – 20:00, there’s usually a “Ladies Night” event with male strippers and horny housewives. They will be found at the booth seats in the main room of the club.

From 21:00 – 23:00, there will be girls dancing on the main stage. Before each girl goes up on stage, you can find her getting ready in the dressing room upstairs. Immediately after each girl gets off the stage, you’ll find her in the Private Dance hallway waiting for a customer.

You can also buy drinks at the bar. However, drinking too much will result in a trip to the clinic.

Arcade

The Arcade is unlockable by simply walking there and entering the building. It is located on the crossroad in the middle of town near the Motel, Library and Townhall. It is also unlocked (if not already) during a main story event to find Tania (Abrahms' daughter) there, plus during Mika's arc, after she challenges you to a game of Air Hockey.

Another way of unlocking content at the Arcade is to complete Chess Club at the University to the point where you've gotten Rosie completely naked. Phillip will send you a text message to come meet him at the Arcade to join in his DnD game. You must meet him at 23:00 on any day.

You will be told that you must buy outfits to wear for both you and Mika, who joins you. Buying the outfits requires having money in your bank account. If you have not yet opened an account, you can do so at the Bank during the daytime opening hours. Once the outfits have been purchased, you must wait for at least one day for them to arrive. When they have, you call Mika so you can try them on.

It is important to note that at this point, you can have sex with Mika in your bedroom while getting changed, but ONLY if you've already had sex at least once before.

Returning to the Arcade with your costumes, you will then have to wait for Mika to come up with a campaign idea. (In development!)

Photo Studio

A cutscene will begin where a young couple come in after the girl was offered a free photo session from Flint, the night before at the club.

This event differs if NTR is on or off.

Morning Bathroom Encounters (Weekdays)

During the morning (07:00 and 08:00), there is a chance that when you try to enter the bathroom in your house, someone will already be in there. If this is the case, you will have 3 options available to you.

 Knock – You knock on the door and whoever is inside will respond according to your relationship score with that person.

 Walk-In – If you do not have a good relationship with whoever is inside, this choice could be quite bad for you. However, different outcomes are available with higher relationship scores.

 Leave – You do nothing.

There is a 40% chance upon entering the bathroom to find someone inside. It can be any one of the girls or even Lucas if he is in the house.

Sex & Pregnancy

Once you have had vaginal sex with a girl who is capable of getting pregnant by you (not everyone can), you will be presented with a few options. Firstly, if you cum inside while the girl is not protected by contraceptive pills, you will be given the choice to not only buy some for her but also give her an emergency morning-after pill to avoid her getting pregnant from that encounter. If she’s already on the pill (or pregnant), you have nothing to worry about. Contraceptive pills last for one week. After that, you’ll need to buy her some more to continue having safe sex.

If she is not on the pill and you don’t provide her with the cash to buy a morning-after pill or give her one yourself within a week, she can become pregnant! Relationships tab notifications will update to show contraceptive or pregnancy status.

A note about pregnancy itself: Pregnancies are not visually noticeable until much later in the game. They mainly affect how the game ends, but also add some content. If a girl is pregnant, she’ll no longer mind if you cum inside of her.

Once any housemate becomes pregnant, there is a chance of morning sickness scenes.

Buying Drugs and Rolling Joints

After unlocking the Cinema during the Main Story, you’ll be able to visit Bear there in the evenings. He sells weed and ecstasy tablets. Rolling papers can be bought from the Petrol Station. Once you have both items, you can click either to roll 3 joints.

You can sell joints to the campers on the Beach (21:00–02:00) or hang out for extra scenes.

As of v0.4.0, you can sell joints to skaters at the skate park at 15:00.
